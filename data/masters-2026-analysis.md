## Augusta National — What Wins Here

Augusta rewards a specific skill set. Get this wrong and you'll be fading the wrong players.

1. **SG: Approach (#1 predictor)** — 150-200 yard iron play is decisive. Every par-5 puts you in mid-iron range. The greens are so fast that position matters more than distance.
2. **SG: Putting** — Fast, severe slopes. Amen Corner and 16 separate contenders from also-rans. You need to putt *well*, not just average.
3. **Distance** — Helpful on par-5s (2, 8, 13, 15) but not a necessity. Augusta is not Torrey Pines.
4. **Augusta experience** — First-timers routinely leave shots on the course reading greens wrong. There is a real experience edge.
5. **Mental composure** — Back-9 Sunday at Augusta is like nowhere else. Amen Corner (11-13) and the stretch home (15-16) create chaos every year.

## Head-to-Head Matchup Targets

This is where the real edge is. H2H removes field noise — you just need one player to beat one other player over 4 rounds.

### 🔥 TIER 1 H2H VALUE — Strong Opinion

**1. Rahm over DeChambeau (same price, both +1200)**
- 2023 Masters champion vs. a player who has historically struggled at Augusta
- Rahm's iron play and course comfort are dramatically superior
- If the book posts this matchup, HEAVY lean on Rahm
- Edge: RAHM

**2. Spieth over Cameron Young (+4000 vs +2200)**
- Spieth is an ~1.8x underdog (+4000 vs +2200) but has won here and been runner-up twice
- Young has zero Augusta track record
- Augusta is where Spieth's career comes alive. Young is a flat-track bully
- At these relative prices, Spieth is a strong lean
- Edge: SPIETH

**3. Matsuyama over Morikawa (+4000 vs +2800)**
- Matsuyama is a past champion at this exact venue
- Morikawa's precision iron game is real but Augusta's creativity and putting demand are not his strongest suit
- Matsuyama is a 1.4x dog to Morikawa — that's wrong
- Edge: MATSUYAMA

**4. Åberg over Morikawa (+1600 vs +2800)**
- Åberg finished T2 in his Masters debut — one of the most impressive Augusta debuts ever
- Morikawa has never contended at Augusta specifically
- Åberg is the better price AND the better Augusta fit
- Edge: ÅBERG (and he's the FAVORITE in this matchup)

**5. Reed over Hovland (+3500 vs +4000)**
- Reed won here. Augusta is HIS tournament mentally
- Hovland's 2024 was rough, form rebuilding
- Reed prices at a discount despite proven Augusta performance
- Edge: REED
