Jalen Duren Reb, Tobias Harris Assists, Jalen Brunson 3PM) were displayed by /api/picks
all day on 3/26 but do NOT appear anywhere in /api/picks/history for 3/26.

- /api/picks/history?date=2026-03-26 returns 13 items — ALL PGA picks, zero NHL/NBA
- /api/picks/history?date=2026-03-27 returns 13 items — ALL PGA picks, zero NHL/NBA
  (today's live NHL/NBA picks are also not persisted yet)
- Compare: /api/picks/history?date=2026-03-25 returns 6 items — 3 NHL + 3 NBA, all resolved

This means 6 picks from 3/26 were shown to users but never recorded. They will not resolve,
not contribute to performance tracking, and the record is lost.

Likely culprit: commit a68e8ee "feat: wire end-to-end auto-grading for Goose AI Picks Model"
— this is the only commit since 3/25 that touched the picks/history path.
The picks route calls storeDailyPickSlate() which may now be throwing and falling back
to the unrecorded "degraded" path. Today's NHL/NBA picks have the same problem.

Priority: HIGH. The history DB is the source of truth for pick performance. If NHL/NBA
picks are not persisting, the entire tracking system is silently broken.
